Shree Swaminarayan Temple

Shree Swaminarayan Templen

The BAPS Shree Swaminarayan Temple in Pune is a Hindu temple built by the Bochasanwasi Akshar Purushottam Swaminarayan Sanstha (BAPS) organization. It is located on Narhe-Ambegaon road and is characterized by intricate carvings and artwork. The temple complex includes a main shrine, a prayer hall, a cultural center, and a vegetarian restaurant. The main shrine houses the deities of Bhagwan Swaminarayan and his divine abode, Akshardham.

    The temple was inaugurated in August 2007 after only 17 months of construction time utilizing 1.3 million volunteer hours.

    Visitors can tour the temple on their own with guidance from the temple staffs at the visitor center.

    Best time to visit

    The Visit Timing for Shree Swaminarayan Temple is 7:00 am - 12:00 noon & 4:00 pm - 8:30 pm
