Harihar Fort

Harihar fort / Harshagad is a fort located 40 km from Nashik City, 48 km from Igatpuri, 40 km from Ghoti in Nashik district, of Maharashtra, India. It is an important fort in Nashik district, and was constructed to look upon the trade route through Gonda Ghat.

KalsuBai

Kalsubai is a mountain in the Western Ghats. Kalsubai is Highest Peak of Maharashtra's Ahmednagar District in Akola Taluka. It known as The Everest of Maharashtra. Its summit, situated at an elevation of 1,646 metres (5,400 ft), is the highest point in Maharashtra.
