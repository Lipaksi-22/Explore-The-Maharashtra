Places In Dhule

Laling Fort

This Fort is known for an old and ruined fort occupying the top of the hill. It is a place of considerable antiquity and the fort is supposed to have been built during the region of Malik Raja Faruqui. Which subsequently granted to has eldest son.

Gurudwara

Gurudwara is known as the largest prayer place for Sikhs. The Gurudwara in Dhule district is also known as the second largest Gurudwara of Sikhs. It is known as one of the biggest and most important inspirations for the Sikh community.

Rajwade research Board Museum

Rajwade Research Board also recognizes it as the premier museum in the Dhule district. V. K Rajwade has established this object museum. Rajwade Research Board and Artifacts Museum were established on January 9, 1927.

Aner Dam Wildlife sanctuary

Aner Dam wildlife Sanctuary located at Shirpur in Dhule District is also known as a favorite tourist spot. There is a river called Aner near this tourist spot. The sanctuary is known as Aner Dam Sanctuary after the name of this river.

Songir Fort

Songir Fort is an ancient historical tourist spot. Songir fort is also known as Suvarnagiri. This fort is located in Songir villages of Dhule district. This fort can be seen near the Mumbai-Agra route.

Ekvira mata mandir

Ekvira Mata Mandir is one of the major religious tourist places in the Dhule district. Among the major religious tourist spots in Dhule district, Ekvira Mata Mandir is also known as the most famous tourist spot.
