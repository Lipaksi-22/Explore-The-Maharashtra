Temples In Pune

Dagdusheth Halwai Ganpati Temple

Built in 1893, Shreemant Dagdusheth Halwai Ganpati Temple is a revered shrine in the city. The shrine sees a huge footfall every day and is undoubtedly one of the most famous Pune tourist places.

Shri Swaminarayan Temple

The glorious Swaminarayan Temple is a treat to the eyes as it is to the soul. The pristine white structure of the temple and the beautiful carving on the walls can leave anyone mesmerised.

Hadashi Temple

The tiny hill station is quite popular amongst devotees of Lord Kirshna for its Sri Satya Sai Pandurang Temple which is an absolute piece of art. it has idols of Lord Krishna as well as Sai Baba in one premises which is a unique sight in itself.
