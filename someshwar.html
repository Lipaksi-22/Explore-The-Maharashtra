Someshwar temple

his is one of the bigger temples of Shiva located on the banks of Godavari. It has a serene campus and one entrance is from the riverside. There is a small waterfall near the river called Someshwar waterfall. The place near the fall is littered with garbage and needs the attention of authorities. It has the potential to develop into a very nice picnic spot.
