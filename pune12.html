Hadashi Temple

Built by Shri Satya Sai baba in 2009, this beautiful temple of Pandurang is situated at Hadshi & spread on 300 acres surrounded by lush green mountains, this is a must to visit place with family and kids for Weekend picnic.

    Lawn is spread over 4 acres of land. Very beautiful landscaping is done. This temple is made for Lord Vitthal, Rukmini and shree Ganesh.

    It is a very beautiful place surrounded by mountains & greenary.

    Best time to visit

    Visit this place duringMansoon season. This temple open from Morning 7am to Late night 8pm daily.

How to reach the Hadashi Temple?

From Airport

Pune International Airport is nearest airport.

Train, Bus –

Pune Railway Junction Station is nearest Railway Satation.

Taxi Drive –

To reach Hadshi there are two routes one is from Chandani Chouk to Pirangut to Paud to Hadshi & other is from old pune-mumbai road, you have to come to pavana dam road then near pawana dam take a left turn to reach Hadshi. Hadshi is total 47km from Pune.
